The problem

Every yes is a bet. Most teams can't see the odds.

When you run a 5–15 person firm, capacity lives in spreadsheets, Slack threads, and your head. You only see the problem after someone burns out or a client complains.

01

No forward visibility

Task boards show what's in progress. They don't show you whether your team can absorb the next project you're about to sign.

02

Shared resources, no system

Your senior designer or lead dev is on three projects. There's no single view of that. Conflicts surface as surprises, not warnings.

03

Reactive, not proactive

By the time you notice someone is underwater, you've already overpromised. Fixing it costs margin, morale, or client trust.

How it works

Built for how small firms actually operate

Not a project tracker. Not a task board. Coordn8r is a capacity planning tool — purpose-built for firms with shared people and real deadlines.

📊

Capacity Command Center

See your entire team's allocation in one view. Overload warnings surface before they become problems — not after. Color-coded by severity: blue, amber, red.

🤖

AI-Assisted Project Planning

Describe a new project and Coordn8r drafts a resource plan for you. See its capacity impact before you commit. Edit what you want, accept when it looks right.

👤

Team Member Profiles

Every team member has a role, availability, and a real-time view of their upcoming weeks. No more checking in to find out who's available.

📅

Timeline View

See projects side by side across time. Spot overlap, tight windows, and gaps at a glance. Make scheduling calls in seconds, not Slack threads.
