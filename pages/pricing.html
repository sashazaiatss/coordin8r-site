FAQ

Common questions

Do I need a credit card to start? +
No. The Starter plan is completely free and requires no credit card. You only need payment details when upgrading to Team or Studio.
What counts as a "team member"? +
Anyone with an account in your Coordin8r workspace. Contractors and part-time staff count. View-only guests do not count toward your limit.
Can I change plans later? +
Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at your next billing cycle.
Is there a setup fee or onboarding cost? +
No setup fees on any plan. Studio includes a complimentary onboarding call to help you configure the tool for your team's workflow.
What happens to my data if I cancel? +
Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation. You can export everything at any time. After 30 days, it is permanently deleted.
