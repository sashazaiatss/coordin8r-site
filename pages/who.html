Fit criteria

Who fits, and who doesn't

Coordin8r is deliberately narrow. It's best for a specific kind of firm.

Dimension Good fit Poor fit
Team size 5–15 people 1–3 (too small) or 20+ (likely already have a solution)
Revenue model Project-based, scoped deliverables Transactional, subscription, time-and-materials
Work structure Multiple clients, shared specialists, real deadlines Single-client, fully dedicated teams per project
Planning maturity Whiteboard planning, spreadsheets, or nothing Existing enterprise PM stack (Workday, Planview, etc.)
Economics Avg. project value >$10k, labour >60% of costs Low-margin, high-volume transactional work
