Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Coordin8r ("we", "us", "our") operates coordin8r.com. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account, including your name, email address, and company name. We also collect usage data to improve the product.

How we use it

  • To operate and improve the Coordin8r service
  • To send product updates and notifications you've opted into
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To process payments (handled by Stripe — we do not store card details)

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. We do not use it to train third-party AI models without your explicit consent.

Data retention

Your account data is retained while your account is active. After cancellation, data is retained for 30 days and then permanently deleted. You can request export or deletion at any time by emailing hello@coordin8r.com.

Cookies

We use cookies for authentication and basic analytics. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use SOC 2-compliant infrastructure.

Contact

Questions about this policy: hello@coordin8r.com
